Exclusive Access

Droppers are the only role authorized to transport Dry Cleaning orders. (Full-Service contractors in "Dropper Mode" are limited to Wash & Fold and Airbnb transport only).

  • Mandatory training on pickup/drop-off procedures.
  • Deactivation dispute protocols included in orientation.
  • Must maintain high acceptance rate for priority orders.

  • Valid Driver's License & Vehicle Insurance.
